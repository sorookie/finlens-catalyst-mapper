# Terms of Use

FinLens Catalyst Mapper is provided for research and analysis workflows.

## Boundaries

- It is not personalized investment advice.
- It should not be used as the sole basis for a trading or investment decision.
- Users remain responsible for verifying facts, timestamps, market scope, units, and source quality.
- Users remain responsible for complying with local laws, regulations, and the terms of any third-party data providers they configure.

## Data and model limitations

- The plugin may rely on public sources, host-provided tools, and optional user-configured providers.
- If key facts cannot be verified, the correct behavior is to stop the forward inference and say so clearly.
- Estimates and judgment should be separated from confirmed facts.
